What Is Debt Restructuring?

Debt restructuring is the process of re-negotiating loan terms with your existing lender (banks or NBFCs) to make repayments more manageable and aligned with your current business capacity. This typically includes:

Enhancing the existing loan limit

(Term Loan or Working Capital)

Reducing the rate of interest

on existing loans

Realigning repayment tenure

or EMIs to suit cash flows

Our core focus is helping businesses retain their banking relationships, while restructuring smartly to reduce cost and increase liquidity.
