Raj Sharma is a seasoned finance professional with over 22 years of cross-industry experience spanning banking, fintech, and real estate. Having worked with some of India's largest financial institutions like ICICI Bank, Kotak Mahindra Bank, IndusInd Bank, and Yes Bank, Raj's journey is marked by continuous innovation, excellence, and thought leadership.
