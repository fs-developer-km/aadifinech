Our End-to-End Foreign Business Setup Services

Company Incorporation & Structuring

We help you register your entity in India, selecting the right structure:

  • Private Limited Company (Wholly Owned Subsidiary)
  • LLP (Limited Liability Partnership)
  • Branch Office / Liaison Office
  • Joint Venture or Strategic Tie-Up

We handle: DIN, DSC, MCA filings, RoC registration, PAN, TAN, GST registrations

Regulatory & Legal Compliance Advisory

We ensure you meet all Indian statutory and legal requirements:

  • RBI Approvals for FDI (Foreign Direct Investment)
  • FEMA (Foreign Exchange Management Act) compliance
  • ROC, Income Tax, and GST-related filings
  • Drafting of MoUs, NDAs, employment contracts
  • Shareholder agreements

Infrastructure & Operational Setup

We help you set up the base of operations in India:

  • Assistance in leasing, interior setup, and utilities
  • Support in sourcing local vendors, logistics
  • Banking partnerships

Employee Hiring & HR Support

  • Talent search for core team members
  • Payroll setup, HR policies
  • Compliance with Indian labor laws
  • EPFO, ESIC registration
