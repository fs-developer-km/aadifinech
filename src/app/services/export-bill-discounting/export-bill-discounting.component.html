Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between discounting and negotiation?

Discounting: Treats bills like loans (private financier does not involve buyer) offering confidentiality
Negotiation: Uses LC-backed conditions and banks confirm documents directly

Q: Recourse vs Non Recourse?

Recourse: Exporter liable if buyer defaults
Non Recourse: Financier bears buyer default risk; usually higher fees
